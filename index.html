Create a Map (<5 min)

Create an account. Copy and paste data points from your excel spreadsheet into our tools. We do the rest for you! Translating addresses into latitude/longitude pairs, generating the map, creating tool tips. All you have to do is share your data.

Use your data!

Addresses in a spreadsheet are useless. Buffalo Open GIS helps you visualize your data and access it from your mobile device. Share your maps with your community, update them in the field, see the patterns in your data. All for free with our easy to use website.

Share your map or embed into your site

Integrating your custom map in your website is as simple as copying and pasting. Don't have a website? Just send the link - we'll host it for you.


WHAT IS BUFFALO OPEN GIS?

Buffalo Open GIS is a system developed to help individuals and community organizations map their data enabling positive impacts on the city of Buffalo. When we looked at the data sets provided by organizations for the WNY Civic App Challenge we saw a common need for groups. The NGOs had spreadsheets full of addresses and latitudes/longitudes, but they didn't have a good way to create, update, and share, that data with other citizens. Building this tool gave us a chance to stretch our abilities and try out some new development tools.

Algonquin Studios believes strongly in giving time and other resources to the Western New York community, especially the City of Buffalo and some of its underserved neighborhoods. If you enjoy this application and plan to use it, please consider supporting another program we operate: Algonquin Sports for Kids: Buffalo Soccer Club. A youth soccer program based on the concepts of healthy choices, personal responsibility, and respect for self and others.
